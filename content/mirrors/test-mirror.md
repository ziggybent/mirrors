## The Nature of Reflection

When we look into a mirror, we see ourselves reversed. This simple act of reflection has profound implications for how we understand our place in the world.

### Physical Properties

Mirrors work through the principle of specular reflection, where light bounces off a smooth surface at equal angles. This creates the illusion of depth and space beyond the mirror's surface.

### Psychological Dimensions

Beyond the physical, mirrors serve as powerful metaphors for self-awareness and introspection. They force us to confront our own image and consider how we present ourselves to the world.
